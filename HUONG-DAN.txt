YHCT ON THI - PHAN TRAC NGHIEM

1. Giu nguyen ca thu muc "YHCT-on-thi" khi chep sang dien thoai hoac may tinh.
2. Mo file index.html bang trinh duyet. Khong can Internet.
3. Ket qua, cau sai va cau danh dau duoc luu tren thiet bi dang dung.

Noi dung hien co:
- 325 cau trac nghiem, 13 bai.
- Hoc toan bo, lam ngau nhien 25 cau, hoc theo bai.
- Cham dung/sai ngay sau khi chon dap an.
- On lai cac cau da lam sai; danh dau cau can nho.
- Tai lieu on tap mo offline trong app.
- 14 case lam sang: case co ban va case nang cao co phoi hop.
- Moi case co ghi nhap tu luu va loi giai tung buoc.

Luu y: Day la cong cu on tap. Phan phuong thuoc va ky thuat dieu tri duoc trinh bay
theo tai lieu hoc phan de luyen thi, khong thay the tu van/chi dinh dieu tri.
